Research Overview

Our group focuses on collective behavior, emergent intelligence, and bio-inspired algorithms. We explore how to train agents to produce complex collaborative, cooperative, adaptive behaviors for optimization, robotics, and distributed systems.

Swarm Algorithms

Studying collective motion and self-organization in multi-agent systems.

Swarm Interactions

Coordinated manipulation and transportation in robotic swarms.
